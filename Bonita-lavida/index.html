About Us

Set in Mallorca, a gorgeous secluded island just off the coast of Spain, Bonita La Vida is a one-of-a-kind all-inclusive getaway and event location that welcomes athletes from all over the world. Translating to “beautiful life” in English, Bonita La Vida is more than just a name to us, it is a personal philosophy. We believe in using our knowledge, passion and dedication to create a sportsperson’s haven where incomparable memories are made. Whether you are visiting us to rest and recuperate, to participate in a sporting event / conference or simply to focus on yourself and define your goals, we are able to cater to your every need with the utmost professionalism and skill.

Join the growing community of athletes and sports enthusiasts who consider Bonita La Vida to be a home away from home and find acceptance, camaraderie and encouragement among your peers.

Are you ready to achieve your personal best?
Bonita La Vida is waiting to welcome you!

Testimonial

Elisabeth Stuhr

Physiotherapist, founder of STAS Fitness Centre & ultramarathon runner

I became interested in long distance cycling in 2019. At the time, I had no experience with this sport, and I was a blank canvas. I contacted the Stayer Coaching Group and cycling coach David Broo. Here, I met a highly dedicated man, who had a broad range of professional experience and considerable knowledge and understanding of training. The unique thing about David as a coach is that he has an unparalleled way of giving his all in his work. He helped me find the right balance between training and everyday life. I gradually achieved a good and steady progress in both strength and conditioning. I learned that training is not just training. It is a harmony of multiple factors. This is valuable knowledge that I will clearly benefit from throughout my life.

Ingvar Straume

59 years, Norway, cyclist

To me, David is a great coach. I started working with David after a period of self-coaching, using online training programs. I have had the pleasure of working with him for some years now and he has really delivered at all levels. He has guided me to a completely different level of performance, not just in terms of producing power, but more importantly to become a better and more confident cyclist on all dimensions. He is genuine, knowledgeable and cares about his athletes. During these years I also joined some of his training camps which have been a great adventure and given me many good experiences and delightful moments (as well as hard days accepting suffering – as David puts it). I have recommended David on many occasions and will continue to do so.

Petter Strømme

70, master cyclists, tennis player, Oslo, Norway.

I travelled with David Broo as head coach to several cycling camps, both in Norway, France and Mallorca. The major goal of these camps has been to increase the athletic performance of the individual participant and I feel that this goal has been achieved each time. Usually, the participants can choose between different levels of training load (duration, speed, elevation, and distance) which suit their own ambitions. David also offers smart advice to cyclists who want to participate in amateur races. This helped me to compete in two difficult races, each with several thousand competitors in a satisfactory way. L’Etape du Tour in France and Trondheim.
