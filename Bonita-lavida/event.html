Event

Receiving an invite to an activity-based event on a secluded island may seem like a dream come true to an attendee, but for the event planner responsible for organizing this event, it could prove to be a logistical nightmare. Fortunately, the full-service events venue at Bonita LaVida has been designed to make even the most complicated events a breeze toplan and execute.

SITUATED IN MALLORCA

Situated in Mallorca, a paradise island off the coast of Spain, Bonita La Vida lends itself particularly well to sporting events, as it is already popular with a passionate global community of cyclists and athletes. The venue is also fully equipped to serve as the location of an all-inclusive company retreat. Some of the amenities that event attendees are sure to appreciate include comfortable accommodation and high-end services, performance enhancing meals, massage therapy, bicycle rental, guided bike tours, state-of-the-art conference facilities, top-class performance coache sand, of course, the breath-taking views that make Mallorca a must-see destination.

Nothing could be easier than organising a sportingevent at Bonita La Vida, as our expert event planning team have gone out oftheir way to make this process as simple and convenient as possible. Ourinnovative online event planning portal has been designed to automate parts ofthe planning process in the following ways:

  • Event planners have the option to steer their event themselves or to make use of our expert team of planners and marketers
  • All information regarding the event is captured on an easy-to-follow online form in less than 15 minutes.
  • Event planners receive targeted marketing material for their events, which can also be sent out directly via the Bonita La Vida marketing chain. This marketing chain includes a wide network of sporting enthusiasts around the world.
  • The Bonita La Vida online system is free for event planners to use in order to sell their events to their clients.
  • All payments related to the event are handled by the Bonita La Vida team, including the event planner’s salary.
  • Event planners also stand a chance to earn extra Euros through Bonita La Vida’s incentive program, which rewards planners for bringing international events to Mallorcan shores.

CHOOSE THE BEST VENUE

Choose the best venue for your event, your attendees and your peace of mind when you choose Bonita La Vida. Our expert event planning team is looking forward to working with you!
