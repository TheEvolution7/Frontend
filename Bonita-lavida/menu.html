THE CAFÉ BONITA LA VIDA

The Café at Bonita La Vida is more than simply a place to have a meal. Some would describe it as a miniature Olympic Village, where the top athletes in their fields gather to refuel, touch base and swap stories of their trials and triumphs. We consider our Café to be the beating heart at the centre of our cycling community, a warm and inviting meeting place where all athletes feel supported and included and where they can swop top training tips with their peers.



Since we are focussed on nurturing every athlete’s personal best, it goes without saying that the food served at our Café is engineered to fuel greatness.Whether you need speed, stamina or strength, our menu will have a tailor-made offering that combines mouth-watering flavours with the latest in performance enhancing nutritional science in order to help you reach your personal goals. You can trust our expertly trained chefs to provide everything you need in order to beat your best and set a new personal record.If it's true that you are what you eat, then make sure you stop by our Café and order a plate of excellence!
