An experience for all age groups Children and adults alike!

Santa is available online at www.AskSanta.com. Since it’s the holiday season and his workshop is working around-the-clock, Santa is ready to talk to you any time of day, any day of the week!

How to speak with Santa in the 21st century:

1) Sign up and create an account (thanks Mom & Dad or over 18 years of age friend)

2) Watch the introductory video (to learn more!)

3) Be sure to always start your questions with “Hey Santa!” so that Santa knows you’re talking to him and can hear you.

4) Remember to speak clearly – it’s a long way from your house to the North Pole!

5) Don’t know what to ask Santa? No worries! Click/tap the pictures to ask the most common questions he gets asked all the time.
