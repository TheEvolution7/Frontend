About Ask Santa

Ask Santa is an online interaction with Santa designed for families that have children of all ages, but especially for those in the magical 3-12 age range. Ask Santa is a web app for use in any web browser with desktop, tablet, and mobile versions. It provides an opportunity for children to have a fun and exciting interaction with Santa, who was filmed in live-action answering questions using StoryFile’s cloud-based AI system. The technology allows for a direct and natural conversation between Santa and the user.

Ask Santa is free, as the StoryFile team wanted to ensure each and every child has the opportunity to experience the holiday magic this year, regardless of circumstance. Donations are encouraged and will benefit children’s charities and philanthropic endeavors.

Ask Santa will be live from Thanksgiving – Christmas. Santa was filmed at his Grotto in the North Pole, and recently completed renovations on his red sled.

About StoryFile

In 2020, the Covid Pandemic will prevent hundreds of thousands of children from meeting Santa in person. This didn’t sit well with the StoryFile team. StoryFile wanted to use its technology to bring joy, fun, and holiday spirit to children around the world this year. As a global leader in AI technologies that support interactive and conversational video storytelling, the goal was simple: bring AI Santa to town this Christmas! And thus, Ask Santa was born.

StoryFile uses powerful natural language processing with its cloud-based AI-driven interactive conversational video platform. StoryFile’s proprietary Conversa technology platform revolutionizes storytelling, creating an entirely new medium.

StoryFile combined its innovative technology and passion for connecting people with the magic of Christmas and the holiday season. Children and adults alike can speak with Santa directly and ask questions in real-time. It’s like a Christmas miracle turned into reality with this industry-game-changing Santa.

To learn more, visit www.StoryFile.com.
