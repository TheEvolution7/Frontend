Save Your Fired Brass & Ditch the Bulky Bag!

On the range or in the field saving your brass has never been more important. Brass Goat by Magwell Mounts is an innovative AR-15 firearm brass catcher, unlike anything else on the market.
Whether you want to easily collect your brass for reloading, or just want to deflect brass while shooting, Brass Goat has you covered.

Explore

Our Features

Streamlined Profile

Our proprietary streamlined design is engineered to direct the flight pattern of fired brass without jams.
Unlike traditional brass catchers Brass Goat does not add extra bulk and minimal weight.

Patented Rapid-Attachment Design

The Brass Goat (BG15) mounting bracket was designed for speed and ease of use.
Engineered for mil-spec lower receivers, it snaps onto the lower receiver of your AR magwell in seconds.

Flexible Design

Brass Goat can be used as a 1-piece or 2-piece accessory. The hopper easily detaches with the push of a button.
Easily deflect your brass while shooting, with or without the hopper attachment.
The mini-hopper is now available, sold separately.
