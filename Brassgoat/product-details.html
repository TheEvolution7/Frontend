    The Brass Goat is a brass catcher that is better by design, reflecting an expertly crafted streamlined profile, created by experienced shooters. This AR-15 shell catcher is compatible with milspec lower receivers, and easily snaps onto the magwell of your firearm in seconds. Brass Goat is sturdy, keeps your sight picture clear, and does not jam. Brass Goat can be used as a 1-piece or 2-piece accessory that includes a detachable hopper, holding up to 30 rounds of .223 brass.
