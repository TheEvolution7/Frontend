Double Hung

Home Double Hung
See what makes our double-hung windows the perfect choice for your home.

Double hung windows are ideal in every room of the house since they are easy to open and clean. Both sash tilt inward, while the screen stays in place. Put two or more double hung windows in a row to create an aesthetically appealing look.

  • Roller-Tilt® Balance System
  • Exterior Glazing
  • Cove Sash Profile
  • Triple Weatherstripping
  • Two Surface Mounted Sculptured Sweep Locks and Tilt Latches
  • Dual Vent Locks
  • INNERGY® Thermal Sash Interlock Reinforcements
