FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Delta-8 THC?
Delta-8 THC is a psychoactive cannabinoid that occurs in the Cannabis plant. When consumed, it is known to cause euphoria, relaxation, and increase in appetite. It is an isomer of, the commonly known, Delta-9 THC.
Is Delta-8 illegal?
It is federally legal but prohibited by law in these states: AK, AZ, AR, CO, DE, ID, IA, KY, MS, MT, NV, UT, RI. Be sure to check with your local legislature as laws are constantly being revised.
What's the difference between Delta-8 and Delta-9?
∆8-THC is less potent than Δ9-THC (being about ≈50-66% as potent in-vivo according to most scientific studies on the topic). However, ∆8-THC is widely known to cause lesser negative side effects such as paranoia, and anxiety. Often said to deliver a "milder, cleaner high" compared to it's Delta-9 derivative.
