About us

Our story

We began as 2 delivery drivers with a dream of owning our own furniture stores

Several stores and 25 years later. We had a thought if Elon Musk can put bullet proof glass on his cyber truck, then why the heck can’t we put a lounge chair on a bed? After that we began thinking of anything we could add to a bed to make our customers have a more convenient and comfortable life.

In a sense we our goal is to be the Swiss Army knife of furniture.

  • Premium materials
  • 5 star service
  • Free delivery
  • We give back
  • Best Price
our Mission

Functional furniture is not our only goal.

We also want to help people in need. We already donate 100’s of mattresses each year to the homeless and the less fortunate. And it doesn’t start there Albi Furniture donates a portion of its profits every year and will do so forever!
